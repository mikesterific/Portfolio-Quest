# About

As a Manager / Technical Lead at Scale Computing, I apply my 20+ years of experience and expertise in web interface design and development to create and enhance a complex web interface for HyperCore software, a leading enterprise-level VM solution. I have spearheaded the transition from a custom templating system to Vue and Vuex, boosting system efficiency and user experience. I have also customized a Node.js back-end, integrating with a Thrift DB and establishing a real-time socket connection with the Vue front-end. Additionally, I have implemented Grafana with a Postgres data source for Timescale database, enabling advanced tracking of VM performance and usage metrics. Furthermore, I have successfully decoupled HyperCore front-end and Node back-end from a C++ monolithic repository, reducing complexity and dependency issues.

My passion and innovation for web interface design and development are also reflected in my previous work experiences and achievements. I completed a high-impact contract for RentPath, elevating Lighthouse scores from 41 to 98 and achieving substantial SEO ranking improvements by optimizing Web Vitals and integrating Next.js. I also led the greenfield architectural design for Dell’s digital transformation, focusing on the buyer experience and achieving a 400% increase in page load performance, a 30% increase in conversion rates, and a tenfold reduction in page weight. I pioneered a Micro Front-end strategy, facilitating component sharing across Dell’s digital ecosystem, and conceptualized and prototyped next-generation user experiences, setting industry benchmarks. I was recognized with the prestigious Platinum Star award, a testament to my exceptional performance and innovation. I have multiple patents and publications in the field of content aggregation and assessment, web development, and performance optimization. I am proficient in Vue, React, Angular, Node.js, Grafana, Next.js, and other cutting-edge technologies. I am always eager to learn new skills and explore new challenges in the web interface domain.
